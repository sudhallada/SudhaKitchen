INGEREDIENTS


For Uthappam:
Raw rice - 2 cups
Urad dal - 3/4 cup
Cooked rice - Handful
Fenugreek seeds - 1tsp
Salt to Taste
Vegetables for uthappam:
Red onion - 1 (Medium ,Chopped)
Carrot -1/4 cup (Grated)
Tomatos - 1/4 cup(Chopped)
Green Chillies - 2 to 3(Chopped)
Ginger - 1/2 inch (Finely Chopped)
Coriander leaves - few (Chopped)
Oil - 1 tsp


METHOD

Preparation for dosa batter:

1.   Soak the rice ,urad dal and fenugreek seeds for 6 hours

2.   Grind them to a thick batter along with the cooked rice (Note: Dosa batter should be fairly thick).

3.   Place the mixture in a warm place for 8-10 hours or overnight to let the batter ferment. The fermentation
      is slow in winter and quick during summer. The key to soft and fluffy Uthappam is in the fermentation process.

Preparation for vegetable Uthappam:

1.   Heat an dosa tawa.

2.   Pour a ladel full of the batter on to the hot pan and spread it.(Note: Uthappam should not be crispy or too thick .
      It should be about 1/2 inck thick)

3.  Drizzel a tsp of oil around the uthappam.

4.  Sprinkle the chopped veggies and let it cook.This has to done as soon as the dosa batter is still liquid and not cooked
      (Note: That helps the veggies to the batter) .

5.   Cover it and steam cook for 4-5 mins lowerheat when golden brown trun and cook for another 2 mins

6.   Serve with sambar or chutney of your choice.
